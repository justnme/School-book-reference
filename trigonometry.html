Sum of Sines

The formula for the sum of sines is:

sin(a + b) = sin(a) * cos(b) + cos(a) * sin(b)
Just enter the values for a and b to find sin(a + b).
Sum of Cosines

The formula for the sum of cosines is:

cos(a + b) = cos(a) * cos(b) - sin(a) * sin(b)
Just enter the values for a and b to find cos(a + b).
Double Angle for Sine

The formula for the double angle of sine is:

sin(2a) = 2 * sin(a) * cos(a)
Just enter the value for a to find sin(2a).
Double Angle for Cosine

The formula for the double angle of cosine is:

cos(2a) = cos²(a) - sin²(a)
Just enter the value for a to find cos(2a).
Cosine via Sine

The formula for cosine via sine is:

cos(2a) = 1 - 2 * sin²(a)
Just enter the value for a to find cos(2a).
